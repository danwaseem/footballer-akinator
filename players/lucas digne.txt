Your Player is Lucas Digne

Lucas Digne is a French professional footballer who plays as a left-back for Spanish club FC Barcelona and the France national team. Digne is a French youth international and has represented his nation at the U16, U17, and U18 levels. Digne represented France at the 2014 FIFA World Cup in Brazil, wearing the number 17 shirt, and in the third group game vs. Ecuador, suffered a foul by Ecuadorian captain Antonio Valencia, for which Valencia was shown a straight red card, effectively ending Ecuador's chances of qualifying for the knockout stage.
