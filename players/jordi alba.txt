Your Player is Jordi Alba

As quick as anyone, Jordi Alba is recognized as one of the most attacking left backs in modern football. Jordi Alba signed for FC Barcelona on 5 July 2012 after the Club had reached an agreement with Valencia for his transfer. The Catalan defender was 23 when he joined the Club and was the first signing of the 2012/13 season.
His great performances at Valencia won him a call up to the full Spanish squad in September 2011 and he made his debut under coach Vicente Del Bosque on 11 October 2011 against Scotland. In the summer of 2012 he was one of the stars of Spain's win in the European Championships played in Poland and Ukraine. The following year he played in the Confederations Cup final and in 2014 he was part of the Spain squad that took part in the World Cup in Brazil. 
Jordi Alba made his official debut for the Club on 19 August 2012 in La Liga against Real Sociedad. The match ended with a 5-1 win for the blaugranes.
