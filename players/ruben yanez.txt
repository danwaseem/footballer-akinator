Your PLayer is Ruben Yanez

The keeper from Girona joined Real Madrid in 2010 to add steel to the Juvenil B squad. He has great potential and adapted perfectly to the Whites' dynamic.
 He is a reliable keeper with quick reflexes who is skilful with the ball at his feet. After making his debut in the Segunda División B in the 2012-13 campaign with Real Madrid C, he has now made the leap to the Castilla squad. After graduating to the first team, he made his competitive debut against Cultural Leonesa during the 2016/17 Copa del Rey campaign.
